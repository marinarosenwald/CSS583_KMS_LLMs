This chapter describes the major processes involved in KM: capturing, creating, codi-fying, sharing, accessing, applying, and reusing knowledge. It presents major KM processes from Bukowitz and Williams (2000), Carlile and Rebentisch (2003), Evans, Dalkir, and Bidian (2015), McElroy (2003) and Xu et al. (2010) A comprehensive review of KM pro- Carbunclecess frameworks by Heisig (2009) is included.
